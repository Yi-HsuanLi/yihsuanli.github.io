李宜軒，台灣視覺設計師，擅長平面設計、識別設計、User Interface 設計、插畫設計、攝影，熱愛嘗試各種實驗性的創作及材質的運用，希望發展出更多可能性，此外，對於設計也擁有高度熱情及堅持，希望將每個作品執行到最完善的狀態。作品曾獲台灣遊戲新人獎、新一代設計的廠商特別獎及ADAA決選、韓國k-design award及澳門設計雙年展入圍等，並受邀收錄於computer arts 雜誌中，之後並相繼於松菸及台灣傳藝中心展出，目前就讀於交通大學應用藝術所，研究視覺設計及介面相關設計。 除了設計，hsuan也熱愛跨領域合作，期許設計可以成為人與人之間的橋樑，使生活更加美好，在2013~2014這段時間，曾經籌辦過設計展及主辦五場以上的跨領域合作講座，並籌辦社會創新巴士，以設計思考的角度去解決社會問題，讓社會永續發展。

. LI . YI . HSUAN .
Hsuan(1992) is enthusiastic about experimenting new material and idea combination to create more possibilities; furthermore, Hsuan is passionate you might say, stubborn about designing the highest quality works. Her designs has won Best original game Award of Taiwan Mobile Elite Award, Sponsors Award by 2015 YODA, also was admitted to compete for ADAA and presented in Computer Arts magazine. Her works was later on demonstrated in National Center of Traditional Arts and Songshan Cultural and Creative Park. She is now studying Interface design and Visual design at National Chiao Tung University Institute of Applied Arts. Other than designing, Hsuan also enjoys inter disciplinary cross field design; hoping it might become an interpersonal bridge to create a better society. Between year of 2013 and 2014, she has hosted a designing exhibition and five inter disciplinary cross field seminars, also put together a session in Social Innovation Bus. In those seminars and sessions she demonstrated how to resolve the problems exist in the society with design perspectives in order to make a greater society.
