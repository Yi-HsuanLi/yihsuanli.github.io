SLOW SLOW FESTIVAL/ 緩 緩 生 活 節

Accumulation of TOXINS / 毒素的累積

type | interactive design ( with Quartz Composer)

design | yihsuanli / 李宜軒/ 曾維薇

programer | 曾維薇

date |June . 2015

Linguistic bullying can be sharp weapon. We can not prevent it from happening in our world living. Every bullying is like toxins which can accumulate and kill people slowly. The toxins will spread with our blood to everywhere such as our brain, heart, stomach and finally to all of the body. When the toxins full of your body, it’s already too late to struggle of it. We wanted to describe the confusion and helpless feeling of the people who have suffered from a lot of the Linguistic bullying with colors. Through this project, we want to say that Linguistic bullying is invisible but fatal. We hope people can think about their speaking words well. ​Three sections / There are three sections of this work. First, accumulation of Toxins, hiding in our body. Second,toxins spreading out all of our body by circulating of blood. In the end, we can see people are struggling inside the toxin line, however, we can not do anything to save himself or herself to be free.
