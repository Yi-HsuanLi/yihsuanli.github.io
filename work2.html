海戀/ Sea Fever

海戀/ Sea Fever

type | poster design/illustration

design | yihsuanli / 李 宜 軒

date |June . 2015

關於海與我的故事，就像是JOHN MASEFIELD這首美麗的詩一樣，我將我對於海的想像及感受，用畫面表現出來，與海相比，我們實在太過於渺小，因此我用”孤寂感”來呈現整個畫面，以”靜”去描寫整個氛圍，一艘小船漂流於海之上，陪伴它的是冰山、海鷗，和一顆偌大月亮，用圓包覆了整個空間，以海戀這首詩佐味，整幅畫面安穩寂靜，這就是海和我之間的關係。 I have got inspired from the beautiful poem written by JOHN MASEFIELD. Following the poem, I described the feeling of sea with graphic design. Comparing to the wild oceans, we are thought to be too tiny and powerless in this world. This truth makes us feel “loneliness” which drives me to design the poster. I describe atmosphere of silence. There is a ship on the sea beside the iceberg and the seagull is flying with a big moon on the sky, which makes us peaceful and comfortable.
